Our mission

Our mission here at HyperNova is to provide gamers all around the world with the best bang for their buck, while still being environmentally friendly.

Our history

Hypernova founded in 2015 by two passionate gamers, quickly became a prominent player in the gaming computer industry. The founders, Simon van den Bouwhuijsen and Philippe Karel, shared a common vision to provide top-tier gaming PCs and accessories to enthusiasts and professional gamers alike. Over the years, Hypernova has established itself as a reliable and innovative company dedicated to pushing the boundaries of gaming technology.
